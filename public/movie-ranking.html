3 Good Movies about Anime

A Silent Voice

The story revolves around a boy who once mocked a deaf and mute girl but later regretted his decision and saved the girl from dying.

Dragon Ball Super: Broly

The movie is based on the super-hit manga Dragon Ball.

I want to Eat Your Pancreas

The movie is about how a boy falls in love with a girl who was about to die but never told the truth to the boy.
